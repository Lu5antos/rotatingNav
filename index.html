My name is Lucas Santos and I love traveling. I had the oppportunity to live in europe for three years while i played soccer professionally. During my time there I was able to travel and explore Europe when I wasn't playing. I traveled to France, Netherlands, Sweden, Portugal and some other amazing places, one of which, was the Amalfi Coast.

Positano

The Amalfi Coast is one of the most famous tourist attractions in Italy. It is comprised of multiple cities on the southern most coast of Italy not as, you guessed it, Amalfi. The city I visited during my time there was called Positano. It was absolutely breath-taking. It's literally a city built on a mountain side which then flows into the ocean. One of the more common tourist activities is going out for a drive on the boat to tour the coast. I definetly reccommend it for your next travel destination.
